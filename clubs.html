Personality Development Club

The personality development club aims to make students industry-ready. Nowadays, industries not only require thorough technical knowledge, but also the ability to present oneself and be updated about the current happenings around the globe, ranging from upcoming technologies, trends, social and cultural issues, economic issues and political issues. 'First impression is the last impression.' Personality development group strives to instil self confidence and develop the skills required by the industry.

Personality Development Club Consists Of Following Clubs In It

Art Club

Foreign Language Club

Photography Club

Social Club

The social club of ISETS works for bringing the social awareness among students.It aims at encouraging students to take up tasks that can make a difference in the society.It may include small activities like cleanliness drives or even celebrating a festival. Awareness in any form is welcomed in this committee which can contribute to the well being of the student. May it be a session on women empowerment or entrepreneurship program.
